What is MyHEARTSMAP?

Why was MyHEARTSMAP created?

When you experience difficult mental health symptoms, going to the emergency department might feel overwhelming.

MyHEARTSMAP was created to assess your mental health resource needs, and connect you with the appropriate supports. Without the hassle of first going to the emergency department.

What does MyHEARTSMAP assess?

MyHEARTSMAP assesses the following domains:

H

Home:

Assesses your Home life and home resources

E

Education & Activities:

Assesses your school, work and hobbies

A

Alcohol & Drugs:

Assesses your alcohol and drug use

R

Relationships & Bullying:

Assesses your friendships and support

T

Thoughts & Anxiety:

Assesses your feelings of stress

S

Sexual Health:

Assesses your sexual health practices and knowledge

M

Mood:

Assesses your mood and behaviours

A

Mood:

Assesses present and past experiences of abuse you may have had

P

Professionals and Resources:

Assesses the resources you currently use and ones you may be intersted in

Who can fill this out?

MyHEARTSMAP is highly reliable when filled out by youth aged 10-17 on their own or by parents on behalf of their child aged 6-17.

What should I do with the results?

You can share the results with your parents or healthcare provider, or keep them to yourself. The process is anonymous and confidential.
